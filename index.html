HSBC India & Taj: A Star-Studded Launch

VVS Laxman

Ananya Panday

The HSBC Taj Credit Card launch marked an exciting evening that brought together luxury, indulgence, and the star power of cricketing legend VVS Laxman and Bollywood actress Ananya Panday. Hosted at the Gateway of India in Mumbai, the event celebrated the card’s unique offerings, including exclusive rewards and privileges for its holders.

VVS Laxman shared his enthusiasm for the card’s reward points & reflected on his personal experiences staying at Taj properties, emphasizing the luxurious, unparalleled experiences it promises. Ananya, a frequent traveler with a deep love for fine dining, echoed the card’s appeal, describing it as the perfect companion for her jet-setting lifestyle. For her, the card brings together her favorite passions: travel, gourmet dining, and creating fond memories wherever she goes.

Throughout the evening, the two stars bonded over their shared admiration for Taj’s impeccable hospitality. Their conversation not only highlighted the allure of the Taj but also conveyed how the HSBC Taj Credit Card offers a gateway to luxury, catering to those who value the finer things in life and seek convenience without compromising on indulgence.

Convergence 2024: Shaping the Future of Business at Infosys Mysore

Steve Waugh

Aman Gupta

Infosys recently hosted Convergence 2024 at its Mysore campus, uniting delivery leaders to explore "The Future of Business." This leadership meet offered a platform for insightful discussions, keynote speeches, and networking opportunities.

As part of this dynamic event, cricket legend Steve Waugh and boAt co-founder Aman Gupta participated in engaging fireside chats, inspiring an audience of over a thousand delivery and tech professionals across two action-packed days. While Steve Waugh shared leadership insights drawn from his illustrious cricket career, Aman Gupta inspired the audience with his entrepreneurial journey and strategies for driving innovation in a competitive market.

Convergence 2024 empowered Infosys leaders with fresh perspectives to shape the future of delivery and business excellence.

BMW Excellence Club: FREUDE Celebrates Sustainable Innovation with Chef Himanshu Saini

Chef Himanshu Saini

Chef Himanshu Saini

The BMW Excellence Club continued its FREUDE series in Mumbai, following the Delhi event with Chef Garima Arora. The event featured a thoughtfully curated dining experience by Chef Himanshu Saini of Tresind Studio Dubai, a two-Michelin-starred restaurant, who presented an exclusive seven-course menu with sustainability as its core theme.

Each course highlighted the complete use of ingredients, such as pumpkin—where the flower, seeds, & stem were all incorporated into a single dish—and coconut and mushrooms, which were used comprehensively to demonstrate their natural potential. This menu beautifully complemented the all-electric BMW i7, a symbol of eco-conscious living.

Guests savored a delectable blend of flavors that reflected the meeting point of ingenuity and environmentally responsible luxury, merging sustainability with elegance and innovation.
